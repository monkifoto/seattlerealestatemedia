During my time at Sammamish Highschool, I discovered my passion for photography and began my career in the field. I was offered a sales position at Kit's Camera, where I soon found myself working in the 1-hour photo booth. It was there that I nurtured my love for developing and printing photos. In fact, it's possible that I may have printed some of your own photos during the film era in places like Factoria, Issaquah, South Center, and Klahanie.

Over the years, shooting a variety of events, weddings, commercial products, I came to the realization that my passion is photographing architecture and interiors.

Please don't hesitate to reach out to me using email, call or text.
